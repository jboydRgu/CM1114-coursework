The Opportunities.

What are these Opportunities?

As part of this society we will present to you the opportunities to explore the creative side of movies.
To be more exact we will allow for cameras, mic, lighting and other equipment to be used, in creating your own short films. The cameras we have are not quite the same ones to be found on multiple million pound sets, they are better than the average and above average camera you would find in your local tech store.
This applies to all the equipment we have on hand. To find out more ask at one of our weekly meetings to find out more!
