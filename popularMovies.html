Most Popular

This list was voted by our users as the most popular movies/shows!


1: Top Gun Maverick

A modern day classic, whatever feelings you have towards
Tom Cruise, you have got to admit he does make good movies.
The movie follows the story of an older Maverick, returning for
one final action packed mission. No overuse of CGI, with a tight
and compact story. The movie has good pacing, dialogue, acting and
some phenomonal dog fights what more could you want!



2: John Wick Chapter 4

A very recent release and a great one at that.
The final chapter to the John Wick story. An example of how to do
action at the highest standard. The Story is simple and follows
John fighting his way out off the control of the high table.
The movie may have a long run time, but never leaves you feeling
bored or that its dragging. The movie features some of the most
impeccable fight scenes and will not leave our minds with out a fight.



3: Better Call Saul Season 6

Where to start with this one...
Truly a masterpiece from Vince Gilligan and Peter Gould.
The perfect example of its the journey that matters and
not the destination. With most fans going in knowing the
ending off our beloved favourite characters, the fact
that the show is still able to keep us on the edge of our
seats, is a monumentous achievment. The show brings the end of
the critically acclaimed breaking bad-verse. In a
beautifully shot and expertly written story proves there
is still some hope for the feature of television.
